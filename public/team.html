There are numerous studies that showed the importance of a good night’s sleep. A good night’s sleep is essential to feel fresh, be active in the morning and make progress during the entire day. However, external factors (e.g: lighting, medication), internal factors (e.g: anxiety, body aches) and cultural differences among different societies can affect sleep timing and quality.
Due to these factors and the additional stress and uncertainty from the COVID-19 pandemic, our team decided to produce this app to help people sleep well.

We you hope enjoy it and get the best result!
